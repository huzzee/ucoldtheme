6. What not to bring with you on your trip

Here are a few things that you should not bring with you on your umrah trip, as they will be expensive and unnecessary.

Liquids over 100ml: These cannot be carried in hand luggage so they must either be left at home or checked into the hold if possible (check with the airline first). Liquids include water bottles, shampoo, etc... Toiletries like sunscreen are allowed though; just make sure there's less than 100ml per item before packing them away safely inside your suitcase! Conclusion

Conclusion paragraph: Now that you know what to bring with you on your umrah trip, it’s time to start packing! Make sure to follow these tips for packing your luggage like a pro. First, pack the items you will need during your flight in your carry-on bag. This includes important documents like your passport and boarding pass, as well as snacks and entertainment for the journey. Next, pack heavier items at the bottom of your suitcase and lighter items on top. Finally, use compression cubes or bags to organize everything and save space. By following these tips, you can be sure that your luggage is packed efficiently and will make traveling a breeze.
